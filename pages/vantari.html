Vantari VR

Project Description

Vantari VR is a Virtual Reality application developed on the Unreal engine.

The application is owned by Vantari VR, and is designed to be a training and assessment tool for doctors & hospitals.

It has been publicly promoted by Channel Nine Australia in 2020.

Project Details

  • Made in Unreal
  • PC VR-based simulation application
  • Targeting Oculus Rift S & Oculus Quest
