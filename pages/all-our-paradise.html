All Our Paradise

Project Description

All Our Paradise is a Augmented Reality experience developed on the Unity engine.

The experience was a collaboration with Edison Chen, and based off his existing experience called "HyperDimensional Friends".

The experience was operational from September to October of 2019, at the Chatswood NSW Concourse.

Project Details

  • Made in Unity
  • AR-based experience, using Computer Vision
  • Debuted in 2019 at Chatswood
  • Launched for Willoughby Visual Arts Biennial
